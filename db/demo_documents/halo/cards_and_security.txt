Halo card support and account security

If a customer's card is lost or stolen, they should freeze it immediately in
the Halo app under Card settings. Freezing the card blocks new payments. The
customer should then contact Halo support so the card can be cancelled and a
replacement sent. Replacement cards are free and usually arrive within 3 to 5
working days.

Customers can freeze and unfreeze their card at any time in the app. Freezing
is useful when a card is misplaced because it stops new payments instantly but
does not cancel the card. If the card is gone for good, the customer should
report it to support.

Halo does not use passwords for login. Customers sign in using a magic link
sent to their registered email address. On mobile, biometric login such as Face
ID or fingerprint can be used for extra convenience.

Halo support should never ask customers for full card numbers, PINs, passwords,
or one-time passcodes.
