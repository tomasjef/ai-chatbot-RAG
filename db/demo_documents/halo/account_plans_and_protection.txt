Halo account plans and deposit protection

Halo offers a free current account with no monthly fee. Two paid plans add extra
features: Halo Plus at GBP 4 a month and Halo Premium at GBP 15 a month. Paid
plans include higher savings rates, travel insurance, and more fee-free cash
withdrawals abroad. Customers can change or cancel their plan at any time in
the app.

Eligible deposits with Halo are protected by the Financial Services
Compensation Scheme up to GBP 85,000 per person. This covers money in the
current account and savings pots combined. Halo is authorised and regulated as
a UK bank.

Figures such as fees, rates, and limits are illustrative for this portfolio
demo and should not be treated as live product terms.
