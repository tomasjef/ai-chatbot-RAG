Halo onboarding and savings pots

To open a Halo account, a customer must be a UK resident aged 18 or over.
During sign-up, customers verify their identity with a valid photo ID such as a
passport or driving licence. They also record a short selfie video so Halo can
confirm their identity. This usually takes a few minutes.

Savings Pots let customers set money aside from their main balance for specific
goals. Instant Access pots earn 3.5 percent AER variable interest, paid
monthly. Customers can withdraw from a pot at any time and can create up to 20
pots per account.

Rates may change over time. Halo can share factual product information, but it
should not give personalised financial advice about whether a customer should
save, borrow, invest, or switch plans.
