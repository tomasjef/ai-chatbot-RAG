Halo travel, disputes, and complaints

Halo adds no fees to the exchange rate when customers spend abroad. Customers
receive the standard Mastercard rate. Free plan customers can withdraw up to
GBP 200 from overseas ATMs each month, after which a 2 percent fee applies.
Paid plans include higher fee-free withdrawal limits.

If a customer sees a payment they do not recognise or believes a transaction is
incorrect, they can raise a dispute in the app by selecting the transaction and
choosing Something wrong. They should provide any details they have. Halo will
investigate and, where appropriate, may issue a temporary refund while the
dispute is reviewed.

Customers can complain through the app or by contacting Halo support. Halo aims
to resolve complaints within 3 working days and will always send a written
response. If Halo cannot resolve a complaint within 8 weeks, the customer has
the right to refer it to the Financial Ombudsman Service.
